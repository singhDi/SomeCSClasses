CMSC Classes taken at UMD

University Maryland offers Both BS/MS program on Computer Science and also with CyberSecurity and Data Science specialization track. I am a tranfer student to University of Maryland and it is my fourth semester here at UMD. So, I want to list all the classes I have taken at UMD.

This webpage demonstrates the classes I took each semester here at UMD and the classes I am taking.Some of the classes besides CMSC courses were taken as my upper level concentration and some of the classes were taken as per my interests. For instance, DANC classes were not required for me but I have taken those as I had interest in dance and also had to be full time in order to maintain my scholarship.

As I have an Associate's degree in Computer Science, my general requirements are already satisfied. Besides, I need to complete 12 credits of upper level concentration for which I have chosen Mathematics.

I have also presented list of classes for Data Science track classes as I am interested in it and I can easily see them. And other list presents Math classes that is needed for upper level requirement.
